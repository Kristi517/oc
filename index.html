Why Choose Us?


Learn from home
One-on-one instruction
Laptop & internet paid
Job training since 1991
Great for beginners!

Office Careers provides one-on-one learning that is highly interactive between the student and instructor. Our training is designed to be relaxed and social with no prerequisites for computer skills.

We want the student to enjoy their class time and develop useful skills that they will retain and easily apply in a new career. Students will develop new skills by using them in the coursework activities and continuous reinforcement.

Meet Karen


Karen has been an instructor with Office Careers since 2013. Prior to joining Office Careers, she worked as a legal secretary for 15+ years and taught for 10 years. She holds her master's degree in education.

Karen is a friendly and engaging instructor. She supports the strengths of each individual student, encouraging them to learn in an unpressured environment.

When not teaching, Karen enjoys spending time with her husband and two teenage children, traveling in the San Juans, and working in her garden.
